## Overview

This artifact documents the classic debugging rite in its mature institutional form:
quiet confidence, rising confusion, excessive instrumentation, and a final solution
that appears spiritually unrelated to the previous hypotheses.

## Notable Features

- Three terminal panes, only one of which was relevant.
- A sequence of increasingly personal commit messages.
- A whispered declaration that the problem was "probably caching" without evidence.

> Conservators believe the final fix worked chiefly because the engineer went to make tea.
