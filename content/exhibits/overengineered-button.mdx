## Provenance

Originally commissioned as a simple marketing button, the system acquired a token
layer, semantic size matrix, animation charter, and governance process before any
user successfully clicked it.

## Conservation Notes

The object remains stable provided curators do not ask why `secondaryGhostQuiet`
inherits from `interactiveSubtle`.

## Display Label

The piece is now shown as a cautionary example of what happens when design systems
are treated as nation states rather than practical agreements.
