## Description

The ritual relies on a sequence of increasingly revealing messages placed along the
execution path. Early inscriptions establish continuity. Later ones express a more
personal relationship to the failing branch.

## Conservation Notes

The museum preserves this practice in situ whenever possible, as removing the logs
too early may destroy evidence of the original investigative mindset.
